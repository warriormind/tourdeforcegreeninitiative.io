Why Schools Matter

Schools are ideal platforms for environmental education, reaching students at formative ages and creating ripple effects in families and communities. Our program creates lasting change by embedding sustainability into school culture.

150+ Schools Transformed
50,000+ Students Reached
75% Waste Reduction

Creating Sustainable Learning Environments

Our Green Schools Initiative works with educational institutions to implement comprehensive environmental programs that transform both infrastructure and curriculum. We take a holistic approach that combines practical environmental improvements with hands-on learning opportunities.

  • Schoolyard transformations with native plants and outdoor classrooms
  • Waste management systems including composting and recycling
  • Water conservation through rainwater harvesting and efficient fixtures
  • Teacher training and curriculum integration
  • Student-led eco-clubs and green teams

Measurable Impact

Water Conservation
